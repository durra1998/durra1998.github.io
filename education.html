Thesis Title:

Contingency Analysis of an IEEE 9-Bus System Considering Optimal Location of UPFC Using Artificial Bee Colony (ABC) Algorithm

Research Highlights:

  • Focused on enhancing power system reliability through contingency analysis of an IEEE 9-bus system with optimal Unified Power Flow Controller (UPFC) placement using the Artificial Bee Colony (ABC) algorithm
  • Evaluated system performance under various contingency scenarios, identifying critical lines whose outage significantly impacts voltage stability and power flow
  • Employed the ABC algorithm to determine the optimal location for UPFC installation to mitigate contingency effects
  • Demonstrated through simulations that strategic UPFC placement effectively maintains voltage stability and minimizes power flow violations during contingency conditions
